About Me

Hi!

My name is Sanja Baranji and I live in Novi Sad.

I’m a painter, graphic and web designer. I’m interested in branding and identity, web and mobile design. I am enthusiastic about different types of cultures and arts. I have always been interested in the principles of composition and color, which have a significant role in my work. I am keen on how smart technology and exquisite design can influence a user.

I enjoy spending time in nature where I can find new solutions and relax. In this way, I also make a balance between free time and work. When I don’t have a pen in my hand, then I’m in the fitness, traveling or in the cinema with my friends.
